🌱 Our Work at Kalemie Agro

Building food security and livelihoods through sustainable farming and processing.

🐔 Poultry Farming

Supplying fresh eggs and quality chicken meat for households and businesses in Kalemie.

🌿 Cassava Production

From farm to flour — we cultivate cassava and process it into hygienic, affordable flour.

🍊 Fruit Cultivation & Juices

Growing local fruits and crafting natural juices, supporting healthier diets and reducing imports.

🏭 Processing & Value-Addition

Turning harvests into packaged goods, creating jobs for youth and women in Kalemie.

💡 Impact: By combining farming with processing, we provide affordable food, empower communities, and strengthen Kalemie’s economy.

Poultry-based fertilizer that enriches soil naturally and sustainably. Eco-friendly and highly effective in boosting soil fertility and crop yields.

🧪 Biofertilizer

Microbial-based fertilizer that enhances nutrient uptake, supports plant immunity, and promotes sustainable farming practices.
